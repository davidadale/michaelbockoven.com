ABOUT MICHAEL

Michael is a native of the Dayton area with a lifetime passion for art. Over the years Michael has developed skills in many areas such as drawing, painting and digital design. He has worked for over 20 years with some of the area’s finest design studios. Michael has also spent much of his extra time freelancing for local customers. His specialty is graphite drawings. His work can usually be found on display within one or more of Dayton’s many local galleries.

Michael spends much of his free time volunteering at the Dayton Art Institute. For 15 years he has also acted as an academic volunteer for the Centerville school system.

Michael is available to provide art training, or to create custom portraits, paintings or digital designs.
